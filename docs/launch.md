## Suggested post copy

### Short

MicLock is a free native macOS menu bar app that fixes bad AirPods/Sony Bluetooth headphone sound quality by keeping your Mac on the right microphone. It is Apple Silicon native, Developer ID signed, and notarized.

### Reddit/HN

I built MicLock because macOS kept switching my input to AirPods/Sony headset microphones, which made output quality sound compressed and muffled. MicLock watches CoreAudio input changes and switches back to your preferred microphone, with one primary input and three fallbacks. It is native AppKit, Apple Silicon only, signed, notarized, and has no analytics or network calls.
